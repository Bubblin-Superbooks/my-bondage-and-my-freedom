Why, I believe if a man should brand his horse in this country—burn the initials of his name into any of his cattle, and publish the ferocious deed here—that the united execrations of Christians in Britain would descend upon him. Yet in the United States, human beings are thus branded. As Whittier says—

     ... _Our countrymen in chains,
      The whip on woman’s shrinking flesh,
      Our soil yet reddening with the stains
      Caught from her scourgings warm and fresh_.
